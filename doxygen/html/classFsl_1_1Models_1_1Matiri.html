template<class Derived , uint Sexes, uint Ages, uint Sectors>
void Fsl::Models::Matiri< Derived, Sexes, Ages, Sectors >::initialise ( void  )
inline

Initialise various model variables based on current parameter values

The fish population is initialised to an unfished state by iterating with virgin recruitment until it reaches equibrium defined by less than 0.01% change in total biomass.

Once the population has converged to unfished equilibrium, the virgin spawning biomass, or the virgin recruitment, can be set.
